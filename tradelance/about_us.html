ABOUT US



Tradelance is an online job marketplace platform that connects customers looking to outsource home improvements and general tasks as well as large industrial projects with skilled public professional enthusiasts and expert industrial contractors.

Our Beginning

A place to connect

Tradelance was born as a concept to create a facilitating network for both customers and trade specialists and allow the knowledge and skills of qualified experts to be quickly and effectively put to use for the people who need them. We believe that connecting skilled professionals and customers who require expert services should be made easy and founded Tradelance for the purpose of having a convenient and accessible network for communication between customers and trade specialists who can be commissioned to do a great number of services in many departments.

Our Philosophy

Local help for local people

Sustainable employment opportunities within local communities create stability, reliability, trust and a brighter future. We position ourselves as the go-to service for both clients looking for reasonably priced and vetted local contractors and service providers who would like a format through which to offer their skills and knowledge to the mutual benefit of all.

Our Mission

Spend time doing things you love. We will take care of the rest

We are here to serve local economies and communities within Ireland by providing an all- inclusive solution for people looking for job opportunities and those looking for local freelance contractors and assistants. Tradelance is the premier outsourcing location for finding local professional labor and knowledge with consideration to affordability and proficiency. There's just no better way to find the skills and resources that you need quickly and effectively than through Tradelance.

Signing up with Tradelance creates an excellent opportunity for professional, individual or company contractors looking for additional business channels and revenue streams, as well as for skilled public looking for some extra cash by offering their experience and know-how to appreciative customers on a per project basis. Clients are looking for your skills right now, so sign up and get to work!.


With Tradelance, you are just a few clicks away from connecting with potential clients or service professionals. If you are looking to hire, this site is guaranteed to be conducive in the process of finding contractors to perform menial to large-scale tasks at your home or place of work to a professional standard, so you can concentrate on other important matters and let willing and licensed or qualified pros get the job done for you.

Tradelance now and get connected!
