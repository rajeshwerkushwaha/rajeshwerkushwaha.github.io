HOW DO I CHOOSE THE RIGHT TRADELANCER FOR MY PROJECT?

It begins with writing a detailed job post. Have it written with your ‘ideal’ tradelancer in mind: be speciﬁc to work requirements, project deliverables, skills required, choose the correct category and sub-category and you will receive great proposals immediately. Provide as much information and description about the project as possible and Include & upload existing blue-prints, drawings,measurements, pictures or project requirements documentation with the job post. The more information you provide – the better quality of the proposals you receive.

If you dont want to wait until proposals are submitted, and have not found a suitable provider after our recommendations, use avialable search options to browse and review tradelancers by:

  • Work history
  • Feedback and ratings
  • Portfolio samples
  • Insurance availability
  • Tradelance level
  • Veriﬁed ID and Credentials
  • Education and professional memberships/certiﬁcates

When you ﬁnd someone you like, get connected immediately. We will provide all tools and support required to have your project completed successfully!

WHAT ARE MY PAYMENT OPTIONS?

You can use a multiple payment options on Tradelance. Begin by selecting your preferred method:

  • PayPal
  • Credit Card
  • Bank Account

Please not that you must have a PayPal account to work through Tradelance.
Once you agree on project and set up terms and conditions, you have two options:
1. Set up project speciﬁc milestones and fund each one individually on completion
2. Set up ﬁxed project budget and pay the whole sum on completion

For both options, you are required to fund (either a milestone or the whole project) prior to the work started by the tradelancer. Money will be held in Tradelance Payments Depository until we receive your approval to release funds when you sign oﬀ on the whole project or an individual milestone work acceptance form. You pay your tradelancer using the Tradelance platform. That’s it. There are no additional costs for using Tradelance to manage your job. Instead, tradelance’s service fee of 9% is paid by the tradelancer, meaning they’ll account for this in their price. For example, when your tradelancer submits a €500 proposal, we add our fee to their quote (you’ll receive a bid of €545). When you pay your tradelancer, we deduct our €45 and pass the remaining €500 on to your tradelancer

AM I PROTECTED?

We want to create the safest work environment for you and service providers and this is our priority N1. We, thus, included diﬀerent control measures to ensure your security and piece of mind

ID and Credentials Veriﬁcation
As part of our ongoing eﬀorts to make new site Irelands safest and most trusted workplace, we would like to oﬀer a free identity veriﬁcation (“ID veriﬁcation”) service for both individual tradelancers and company tradelancers. Payment Protection
Tradelance Payment Protection helps ensure that you only pay for work you have received and approved. Created for Fixed Price jobs, you have the ability to ﬁrst inspect work previously agreed upon and delivered by tradelancers, before releasing funds from Tradelance secured Payments Depositary(Escrow) to them.

Dispute Assistance
The vast majority of work on Tradelance is delivered to the complete satisfaction of both the client and the tradelancer. Very seldom, however, a disagreement can occur between you and tradelancers and we will assist you in every way to help ﬁnding resolution
