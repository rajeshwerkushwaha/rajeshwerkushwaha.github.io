HOW DO I CHOOSE THE RIGHT TRADELANCER FOR MY PROJECT?

It begins with writing a detailed job post. Have it written with your ideal Tradelancer in mind: be specific to work requirements, project deliverables, and skills required, then choose the according category and sub-category and you will receive great proposals immediately. It's that easy! Provide as much information and description about the project as possible and include & upload existing blue-prints, drawings, measurements, pictures or project requirements documentation with the job post. The more information you provide—the better quality of the proposals you will receive.

If you don't want to wait until proposals are submitted and have not found a suitable provider after our recommendations, feel free to use available search options to browse and review Tradelancers by:

  • Work history
  • Feedback and ratings
  • Portfolio samples
  • Insurance availability
  • Tradelance level
  • Veriﬁed ID and Credentials
  • Education and professional memberships/certiﬁcates

WHAT ARE MY PAYMENT OPTIONS?

You can choose from multiple payment options on Tradelance. Begin by selecting your preferred method:

  • PayPal
  • Credit Card
  • Bank Account

Please note that you must have a Paypal account.
Once you agree on the project and set up terms and conditions, you have two options:
1. Set up project specific milestones and fund each one individually on completion
2. Set up fixed project budget and pay the whole sum on completion

For both options, you are required to fund (either a milestone or the whole project) prior to the work started by the Tradelancer. Money will be help in the Tradelance Payments Depository until we receive your approval to release funds when you sign off on the whole project or an individual milestone work acceptance form. You pay your tradelancer using the Tradelance platform, that's it. There are no additional costs for using Tradelance to manage your job. Instead, Tradelance's service fee of 9% is paid by the Tradelancer, meaning they'll account for this in their price. For example, when your Tradelancer submits an £500 proposal, we add our fee to their quote (you'll receive a bid of £545). When you pay your Tradelancer, we deduct our £45 and pass the remaining £500 on to your Tradelancer.

AM I PROTECTED?

We want to create the safest work environment for you as well as service providers, and this is our number one priority. Thus, we included different control measures to ensure your security, giving everyone involved peace of mind.

ID and Credentials Veriﬁcation
As part of our ongoing efforts to make Tradelance Ireland's safest and most trusted workplace. We would like to offer a free identity verification service for both individual Tradelancers and company Tradelancers. We also offer credential verification services in cooperation with our partners platforms.

Payment Protection
Tradelance Payment Protection helps ensure that you only pay for work you have received and approved. Created for Fixed Price jobs, you have the ability to first inspect work previously agreed upon and delivered by Tradelancers, before releasing funds from Tradelance secured Payments Depository(Escrow) to them.

Dispute Assistance
The vast majority of work on Tradelance is delivered to the complete satisfaction of both client and Tradelancer. Very seldom, however, a disagreement may arise between customer and Tradelancer, for which we must be prepared and will assist in every way to help finding a resolution.
