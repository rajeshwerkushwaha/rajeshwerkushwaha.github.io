HOW DO I GET STARTED?

To kick off your new career on Tradelance, you only need to create a professional looking profile, and it's free! Make sure to create a profile that leaves a lasting impression. If your profile is drab, customers are inclined to overlook you and move on to the next prospect. With a fully completed and attractive profile you are guaranteed to draw attention and get hired quicker. We give you all the tools you need—the rest is up to you! When making a profile, focus on what you excel at, instead of being a jack-of-all-trades. Customers are looking for unique specialists who can guarantee satisfaction in the particular arrangement they require. Make sure to supply documentation, certificates and references that are authentic and up-to-date—this is pivotal to opening doors on Tradelance, people need to know what you're worth before they hire you! If you are on a free membership, a fully completed profile will also unlock a full set of free monthly invites to apply for jobs, so we encourage you to make sure all requested information fields are complete.

HOW DO I CONNECT AND GET HIRED?

Begin by browsing available jobs in your selected category/ies—once you've found a project that you're interested in, apply by sending a customer a proposal with a well-written cover letter. Fantastic cover letters will significantly increase your chances of being noticed and requested for further interview and correspondence, so be sure yours is:

  • Personalised and job specific—never use a copy/pasted job application or cover letter from your past, make the customer feel appreciated and instill confidence in your commitment!
  • Have a brief explanation on why you're interested in the job
  • Outline qualifications that are matched to the job/project specs
  • Use bullet points to get maximum impact with as few words as possible
  • Specific- if you can include a figure or two, so much the better
  • Thank the person for their time and consideration
  • Sign off with a positive statement to prompt the client to take action
  • Be assertive, make the client realise you are the right person for the job!

Did you find a few jobs you're interested in? Apply to them all and increase your chances of landing work!
